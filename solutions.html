Our Services

Transport Solutions

Tailored logistics operations across East and Central Africa with uncompromising reliability.

Core Service

Containerized Cargo

We specialize in the safe and efficient transport of containerized cargo across East and Central Africa. From the Port of Mombasa to the hinterlands of Uganda, Rwanda, South Sudan, and DRC, our fleet ensures your goods arrive intact and on time.

  • 20ft and 40ft container handling capabilities.
  • Real-time GPS tracking for total supply chain visibility.
  • Port clearance alignment to eliminate demurrage delays.

Specialized

Petroleum Products

Safety is our highest priority when transporting hazardous and flammable petroleum products. Our specialized tankers and highly rigorously trained drivers ensure that your liquid bulk cargo is delivered securely, adhering to stringent HSSE standards.

  • High-capacity tankers designed for safety and stability.
  • Strict adherence to regional environmental and transit regulations.
  • Dedicated emergency response protocols.
Bulk Logistics

Dry Bulk Cargo

We provide efficient and reliable transport for high-volume dry bulk commodities. Our specialized equipment and operational expertise ensure the seamless movement of grains, minerals, and industrial raw materials across the region.

  • Dedicated tipper and high-sided trailer fleets.
  • Expertise in handling grains, clinker, fertilizers, and aggregates.
  • Strict moisture and contamination control protocols.
Heavy Lift

Out of Gauge Load

When cargo exceeds standard dimensions, you need a logistics partner with specialized equipment and technical expertise. We handle abnormally sized or excessively heavy freight, from industrial machinery to construction materials.

  • Low-loader fleets accommodating diverse cargo profiles.
  • Route surveying and permit acquisition across borders.
  • Escort vehicles and comprehensive security measures.
End-to-End

Project Logistics

AEL provides comprehensive, end-to-end logistics solutions tailored for massive infrastructure and energy projects. We coordinate multi-modal transport, warehousing, and staging to ensure materials arrive precisely when the project timeline demands.

  • Dedicated project management and consulting teams.
  • Synchronization of multiple deliveries for zero downtown.
  • On-site coordination and materials handling.
