# AEL Company Profile

## About the Company

Awale Enterprises Limited is a road freight transportation company. Our services cover across East Africa, South Sudan, Rwanda, Burundi, Eastern Congo and Northern Zambia. The company was founded in 2005 by Abdi Awale in South Sudan as a Transportation and Construction company and later incorporated in Kenya as a limited liability company in the year 2006\.

We are competent in a wide variety of transportation solutions including containerised cargo, loose cargo, foodstuffs, abnormal / out of gauge, petroleum products and related custom solutions. Our services are offered at a competitive rate and guarantee service is provided by a robust and varied fleet, qualified staff and unparalleled knowledge of the regions.

## Our Services
The main objectives of the company is to provide cargo transportation services in the East African and neighbouring countries. The services we offer include:
ter
### Containerised Cargo Transport
   We have an extensive fleet of flatbed and skeleton trailer units and also have the business knowledge and intergovernmental regulations knowhow to carry out transportation of both TEUs and FEUs efficiently to any country.

   From the point of loading to offloading, we offer value added services that are comparable to the best practices in the world. Where necessary and especially when it comes to partial container load, we offer our best practices to ensure cargo, crew and truck are safe at all times. Our clients are assured that their containers will be delivered to the destination safely, on time.

### Loose Cargo Transportation
   Loose cargo is always loaded either as bagged or bulk from the warehouse or from the port. Transportation for this kind of cargo calls for timeliness especially from point of loading to destination.To satisfy the specialized handling demands for loose cargo, we have an adequate and fast-growing fleet of tippers and closed body trailers assigned with best drivers.

### Petroleum products (AGO, PMS, LPG, HHK)***
   Petroleum products are hazardous in nature. Moreover, being liquid, the load calls for drivers who are better skilled than the average truck driver. Petroleum dealers expect nothing less than flawless service and efficiency to keep risk at a minimum and meet their highly dynamic business. We have over the years been capable to put in place continuously trained drivers and well maintained trucks to meet their expectations.

### Abnormal / Out of Gauge cargo
   We meet the challenges of transporting abnormal or Out of Gauge cargo. like; factory Equipments, cranes, large pipes, agricultural machinery etc. Our project cargo team have extensive experience to ensure all the necessary permits and regulations are accurate and on time

   We provide the best drivers who are patient and have mechanic experience to deliver the cargo using the safest routes and most conveniently.

### Our Capacity
The company owns 166 modern and well maintained trucks. These include flatbeds, skeletons,high-sided, closed body, low-loaders of different capacities and tippers. We have been able to satisfy varied contracts across the East African region.

The company is managed by two duty directors with one designated as the Managing Director and the other tasked with overseeing operations, marketing and finance matters. The operations department has thirty (57) members of staff undertaking various duties including administration, coordination, clerical work, customer care, accounting and marketing.

To keep our trucks in excellent mechanical condition, we have invested in a modern maintenance workshop equipped with all the requisite equipment. The workshop is runned by twenty (39) qualified and experienced mechanics with various specializations including mechanical, electrical and electronics work.

### Our Fleet
We operate a comprehensive and modern fleet of trucks of various Kinds . Our fleet consists of over 63 High sided trucks, 45 Tipping trucks, 26 skeleton, 17 flatbeds, 6 Tankers and 10 low bed trucks. All vehicles have ECTS compliant and the trucks go through inspection in a yearly basis to ensure they are in road worth condition. 

#### Flatbed Trailers
Flatbeds specialty is carrying non-containerised goods, especially abnormal cargo like machinery, pipes, plant/factory components, etc.

#### Skeletal trailers
Our skeletal trailers are primarily assigned containerised cargo and can either be loaded with 1x40ft or 2x20ft containers.

#### Low loaders
For especially heavy and long cargo exceeding 40ft, we have invested in capable low loader trailers to ensure we remain a one-stop shop for all our clients' transportation needs.

#### Tanker trucks
These are exclusively used to transport petroleum products. They are assigned to our best, most experienced drivers who also bear special qualifications and licences that are necessary for the safe delivery of petroleum products.

#### Tippers
For loads like aggregate, clinker, crushed rock, soil, non-packed fertilizer, grain and similar consignments.

#### Closed Body trucks
Closed body trucks are best suited for transporting loose cargo like non containerised food stuffs, goods packed in cartons and similar consignments.

#### High sided Trucks
High sided trailers are versatile in that they can be used to transport a variety of cargo types including containerised, grain, rock aggregate, metal bars, rods, etc.

### Support Amenities

#### Operations Office
We are headquartered in Mazeras area - about 15 minutes’ drive from the port of Mombasa. This proximity to the port comes in handy when it comes to coordination of loading between our clerks and the operations department. It is also convenient for our customers who may need to physically visit us to iron out details of any contract.

  Each of the staffers is assigned to a modern workstation connected to high speed, dedicated fiber internet connection for inter-departmental communication as well as communication with clients and other stakeholders. Select workstations are installed with custom-made software that we use internally to log, track and keep record of deliveries.

In addition to our headquarters, we also regional offices in Kampala - Uganda and Juba - South Sudan.

#### Yard
Our operations headquarters sits on a 5-acre yard. The yard is expansive enough to hold all our trucks. It is essentially the base on which our trucks pack in readiness for assignments. This facility is under cloud-connected 24-hour CCTV surveillance in addition to manned security inside and at the entrance.

  We also have yards in Nairobi, Kampala and Juba to cater for our cross-border operations.

#### Workshop
To ensure that our trucks are well-maintained and in top form, we have invested in a modern, well equipped, sufficiently staffed repairs and service workshop. All mechanical, electrical or gassing works for our fleet is done in-house by our qualified team of mechanics.
