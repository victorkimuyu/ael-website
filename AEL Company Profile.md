# AEL Company Profile
## Background
Awale Enterprises Limited is a road freight transportation company with services coverage across East Africa, South Sudan, Rwanda, Burundi, Eastern DRC,Malawi and Zambia

The company was founded in 2005 by Abdi Awale in South Sudan as a Transport and Construction company and was later incorporated in Kenya as a limited liability company in the year
2006.

Backed by 2 decades of experience, we are competent in a wide variety of transportation solutions including containerised cargo, loose cargo, foodstuffs, abnormal / awkward loads, petroleum products and related custom solutions. Our services are offered at competitive rates and service guarantee provided by a robust and varied fleet of trucks, qualified staff and unparalleled knowledge of the region we operate in.

## Our Solutions
### Overview
Awale Enterprises Limited has built a stellar reputation over the years, offering efficient and competitively priced cargo transportation services. Governmental agencies, local and international Non-Governmental Organisations and companies of all sizes have over the years relied on us for transportation from the Port of Mombasa to everywhere in East Africa and beyond.

### Containerised Cargo Transport
A big percentage of imports and exports is packed in containers - either 20ft or 40ft in length. Other than having an extensive fleet of flatbed and skeleton trailer units, we have the requisite business knowledge and regional government regulations to carry out transportation of containerised goods efficiently.

From the point of container loading to consignee location, we offer value added services that are comparable to the best practices in the world. Where necessary and especially when it comes to partial container load cargo, we offer our time-tested knowledge on best packing practices to ensure cargo, crew and truck safety. Our clients can rest easy with the assurance that their containers will be delivered to the destination safely, on time and at very competitive rates. Loose Cargo Transportation Loose cargo is cargo whose shape or dimensions cannot be practically packed into a six sided container. Earth moving machinery fall under this category. Others include metal bars, coils and sheets. Transportation for this kind of cargo calls for stringent safety and security measures, from point of loading to destination.

To satisfy the specialized handling demands for loose cargo, we have an adequate and fast-growing fleet of flatbeds, closed body trailers and lowloaders assigned to the best drivers.


### Petroleum products (AGO, PMS, LPG, HHK)
Awale Enterprises, being an established name in the Transportation business along the Northern corridor is the service provider of choice for Petroleum products are hazardous in nature. Moreover, being liquid, the load calls for drivers who are better skilled than the average truck driver. Petroleum dealers expect nothing less than flawless service and efficiency to keep risk at a minimum and meet their internal KPI’s in a very competitive and highly dynamic business. They have over the years relied on us as capable partners who have put in place the necessary trucks and organizational capacity to meet their expectations

### Out of gauge cargo
We meet the challenges of transporting abnormal or Out of Gauge cargo. like; factory Equipment, cranes, large pipes, agricultural machinery etc. Our project cargo team have extensive experience to ensure all the necessary permits and regulations are accurate and on time.

We provide the best drivers who are patient and have mechanic experience to deliver the cargo using the safest routes and most conveniently.

## Organizational Capacity
### Summary
The company owns 172 modern and well maintained trucks. These include flatbeds, skeletals,high-sided, closed body, low-loaders of different capacities and tippers.We have been able to satisfy varied contracts across the East African Region.

The company is managed by two duty directors with one designated as the Managing Director and the other tasked with overseeing operations, marketing and finance matters. The operations department has twentyfive (25) members of staff undertaking various duties including administration, coordination, clerical work, customer care, accounting and marketing.

To keep our trucks in excellent mechanical condition, we have invested in modern meintenance workshops in all our branches (Mombasa, Nairobi, Kampala and Juba). The workshops is run by twenty (25) qualified and experienced mechanics with various specializations including mechanical, electrical and electronics work. Depending on workload and techical considerations, we ocassionaly engage technicians on casual basis to offer needed assistance or techinical support.

Our fleet is assigned to a rigorously vetted crew of 143 drivers. We undertake regular training and continuous assessments of our drivers to ensure that they are always well versed in road safety, government regulations, truck & cargo stewardship and general discipline.

### Key Staff
Our most valuable resource is the group of professionals who make it possible for us to undertake any project with confidence. Organized into departments according to specialty, the management has created the most conducive environmental for inter-departmental cooperation and collaboration.

#### Operations Department
The Awale Enterprises operations department is concerned with coordinating all the activities that entail our engagement with clients on any particular project. In essence this department is the glue that binds all the other departments into a cohesive unit, working together towards the common goal of total client satisfaction.

Operations members of staff include:-

##### Operations Manager
The Operations manager acts as the departmental head. He/she is tasked with ensuring that there is proper coordination, punctual action and accurate correspondence between the company and clients on loading, dispatch and delivery. She is works very closely with the Transport manager in managing cargo delivery logistics.

##### Human Resource Manager
Reporting to the director, the human resource manager is responsible for overseeing staffing, induction, welfare and safety, performance monitoring and employee labuor relations. To achieve our objectives, this is a very significant. We regard our staff as the most valuable cog in the gear system of our operations. This calls for most cordial relationship between the company and the staff so as to ensure individual growth and motivation for peak performance.

##### Workshop Manager
The workshop manager is ideally the head mechanic. His main responsibility is to ensure that our trucks and trailers are in their best mechanical form, guaranteeing reliability and performing truck safety audits and inspections. Working under him is a dynamic team of experts ranging whose competencies range from mechanical to electrical and everything in between.

##### Drivers
Our fleet is assigned to drivers who are only hired after a very rigorous testing and vetting process. The safety and security of our client's cargo is paramount to us. Punctuality is one of our key performance indicators. Drivers, being the people tasked with the safe and punctual delivery of our customers' consignments play a pivotal role in our customer satisfaction efforts.

Our drivers undergo constant performance monitoring, refresher and skills reinforcement courses, safety drills and simulated defensive driving exercises to maintain the highest levels of aptitude. This is in addition to the mandatory professional and ethical induction initiatives at the time of employment.

##### Loading Clerks
Our loading clerks are the liaison officers at the point of loading. They assist on gathering necessary documentation and act as field officers on the ground to coordinate loading with the operations manager. Our loading clerks are well trained on all logistics appertaining to road freight and are an invaluable resource in ensuring that we offer the best customer experience.

#### Accounts department
The financial aspects of our company falls on the shoulders of our wellstaffed accounts department. Among other related responsibilities, the accounts department makes quotations, raises invoices, processes payroll and generally ensures the company's liquidity.

This department is headed by the chief accountant. Reporting to the managing director, the chief accountant is tasked with coordinating and providing leadership on all matters finance. Working under him is a team of academically qualified and experienced accountants and a cashier. Together, they work in close collaboration with the operations and transport departments to ensure that the all financial aspects of our operations are handled in the most professional and courteous manner, measuring up to the best international practices and applicable government regulations.

### Our fleet
Awale Enterprises operates a comprehensive and modern fleet of trucks and various types of trailers. Our fleet consists of 142 units including prime movers, tippers, tankers, lowloaders and assorted trailers. All vehicles and equipment are on a strict and thorough maintenance schedule carried out in-house by our workshop staff to ensure best mechanical condition and adherence to stipulated government standards.

We are consistently growing this vital asset to ensure that we are adequately equiped for current needs and poised to take on future opportunities.

#### Prime movers
Comprising of reliable brands like Mercedes Benz and Scania, our fleet of prime movers are the backbone of our operations. Flatbed Trailers - Flatbeds specialty is carrying non-containerised goods, especially abnormal/awkward loads like machinery, pipes, plant/ factory components, etc.

#### Skeletal trailers
Our skeletal trailers are primarily assigned containerised cargo and can either be loaded with 1x40ft or 2x20ft containers.

#### Low loaders
For especially heavy and long cargo exceeding 40ft, we have invested in capable low loader trailers to ensure we remain a onestop shop for all our clients' transportation needs.

#### Tanker trucks
These are exclusively used to transport petroleum products. They are assigned to our best, most experienced drivers who also bear special qualifications and licences that are necessary for the safe delivery of petroleum products.

#### Tippers
For loads like aggregate, clinker, crushed rock, soil, non-packed fertilizer, grain and similar consignments.

#### Closed Body trucks
Closed body trucks are best suited for transporting loose cargo like non containerised food stuffs, goods packed in cartons and similar consignments.

#### High sided Trucks
High sided trailers are versatile in that they can be used to transport a variety of cargo types including containerised, grain, aggregate, metal bars, rods, etc.

### Value Proposition
#### Capacity
Awale Enterprises is adequately equipped to undertake overland transportation projects of all sizes and complexity. Our staff are qualified and fluent in regional road freight logistics. Our fleet is extensive, varied and well maintained to empower us in delivering cargo effectively, safely and punctually.

#### Experience
With close to 2 decades of front-line local and transit transport operations, we have developed a thorough understanding of the regional business landscape. This enables us to tackle any and all assignments with the confidence and offer our clients the best value in terms of quality, risk mitigation and cost minimization.

#### Cargo Safety
Our well trained and experienced crew of drivers is an invaluable resource. Oftentimes, we are tasked with transporting the goods of high value or critically required cargo like medicine and relief food. This calls for extra special safety considerations to ensure that the cargo reach the consignee in good condition and order. Indeed, regardless of the cargo, we have in place policies and safety checklists that are zealously enforced by the management to eliminate the risk of breakages, weather damage, spillages or pilferage.

#### Compliance
We endeavour at all times to operate our business within the laid out government regulations and laws. We have all the required licences, permits and endorsements to do the road transport business locally and in the region. For instance, all our trucks are fitted with Electronic Cargo Tracking System (ECTS) at the loading point for every trip. The scheduled government fleet inspections are dutifully observed so as to ensure that each and every one of our trucks are ready and compliant for assignment at all times.

#### Regional Coverage
With our footprint spread across the region, Awale Enterprises is well placed to be the preeminent trucker of transit cargo. In addition to our head office and main yard in Mombasa, we have set up regional operations offices in Nairobi, Kampala - Uganda, and Juba - South Sudan. These offices act as coordination centers for our operations in their respective countries as well as liaison points for our clients across the region.

#### Tradition of Integrity and Honesty
We have in place a zero tolerance to corruption, dishonesty and all manner of crookedness. We endeavour to operate a clean business where we treat our clients and other stakeholders in the value chain utmost good faith. This policy is codified in our staff induction procedures and it is part of the company culture familiarization process that new employees must undergo before fully assuming their duties. Further the code of conduct eloquently spells out the highest ethical standards to be observed by our employees when dealing with clients or with each other internally.

#### Innovation and flexibility
The road freight transport business is dynamic. Constant changes in business practices and government regulations all call for continuous assessment and improvement of existing service delivery procedures. At Awale Enterprises, we embrace change as the a catalyst for rejuvenation, innovation and growth.
