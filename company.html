About AEL

The AEL Difference

Over two decades of excellence in road freight and logistics across East and Central Africa.

Our Heritage

Driving East Africa Since 2005

Founded in 2005, Awale Enterprises Limited (AEL) began with a clear vision: to bridge the logistical gaps between the Port of Mombasa and the expanding markets of the East African hinterland.

Starting with a small fleet of dedicated trucks, we have grown into a multi-modal logistics powerhouse. Our journey is defined by a relentless commitment to safety, reliability, and the economic integration of the region. Today, we stand as a preferred partner for global brands and local industries alike.
